Welcome to Elland Road!

Elland Road Stadium holds a special place in the hearts of football fans worldwide as the iconic home of Leeds United Football Club. Nestled in the heart of Leeds, England, this historic venue has witnessed countless thrilling matches, dramatic victories, and unforgettable moments.

For over a century, Elland Road has been the beating heart of Leeds United, serving as the stage where legends are made and dreams come to life. From the roaring chants of loyal supporters to the electric atmosphere on matchdays, the spirit of Elland Road is palpable.

As you explore this website, immerse yourself in the rich history and heritage of Elland Road. Discover the triumphs, the challenges, and the enduring legacy that make this stadium an enduring symbol of pride for Leeds United and its passionate fanbase.
